About Me

One of my first memories as a child was seeing my dad take apart the family computer. I believe he was just adding a hard drive but that was a long time ago and I’m sure my memory fails me. But what I do remember vividly is the feelings I got watching him. The wonder and excitement that bubbled inside. I didn’t know much at that age, but I knew at that point I wanted to explore more with computers, whatever that led to.
Fast forward many years and I found myself meandering. After quite a few years of doing some work that I loved and some work I can’t say the same about, I knew I needed a change. I love working with my hands but I also need to exercise my brain. I needed challenges that the only work I really could find could never afford me. My passion for computers and coding were still there but I never had any attempts at self teaching come to fruition.
Finally, I decided enough was enough. I was tired of wasting my time and I was going to enter the field that I had dreamed of for all these years. So as soon as things slowed down I signed up for a bootcamp and the rest is history. I look forward to bringing my history of nose to the grindstone work ethic from the factory floor and combine it with my passion for development in the many years to come.
Specialties: Full Stack Web Development (Ruby, Rails, Vue.js, HTML, CSS, PostgreSQL)
