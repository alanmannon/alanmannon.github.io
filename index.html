About Me

Thank you for checking out my page! I'm a lifelong lover of technology who is enjoying every step of the way along my long journey in coding. While most of my background isn't in tech, it has been a big part of my life. My whole life my main hobbies have included computers and technology and I've always dreamt of moving into the field professionally. After dabbling in some Ruby and Python courses online I decided to sign up for a bootcamp and have fully enjoyed completely immersing myself into programming. I look forward to take my work ethic and problem solving from the production floor and use it to the fullest extent in my coding.

When I'm not programming, I enjoy spending my free time going to and competing in esports tournaments in the Chicago area. I'm also an avid fan of music and travel all over the Midwest attending concerts.

What to Eat
